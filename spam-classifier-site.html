01 · The pipeline

Three steps between a raw email and a verdict

Nothing here is hidden. The same three operations run whether you're scanning above or training on 800 emails from the command line.

01

Clean

Lowercase the text, collapse every URL to URL and every number to NUM, and strip punctuation — so Click NOW!!! and click now become the same signal.

02

Vectorize

A TfidfVectorizer turns the cleaned text into weighted word and word-pair frequencies — 693 features learned from the training set, unigrams and bigrams alike.

03

Score

A MultinomialNB classifier compares the log-probability of those features under "spam" vs. "ham" and returns whichever is more likely.
